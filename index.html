Research

Ongoing

Deep Reinforcement Learning for Autonomous Multi-Spacecraft Coordination in Proximity Operations

Developed deep reinforcement learning-based frameworks for autonomous multi-spacecraft coordination in congested Low Earth Orbit environments.

  • Implemented Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) within Hill-Clohessy-Wiltshire relative dynamics to enable collision avoidance and coordinated rendezvous
  • Modeled realistic operational constraints including 10-second control update rates, thrust limits, and 20-meter safety zones
  • Compared four coordination strategies using Monte Carlo analysis
  • Demonstrated that transfer learning enables scalable multi-agent coordination without retraining, outperforming classical LQR and MPC baselines
